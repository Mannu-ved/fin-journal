About Us

The personal journey that led to My Finance Journal

My journey into personal finance got triggered after an unfortunate event that pushed me into the water when I was learning how to swim.

I was about to complete my Master's degree in Business Administration with a Finance major when tragedy struck from several fronts. An external financial crisis coupled with personal difficulties created a challenging situation that forced me to confront the reality of financial vulnerability.

This experience became the foundation for My Finance Journal. I realized that understanding personal finance isn't just an academic exercise — it's a vital life skill that can mean the difference between weathering a storm and being swept away by it.

Our Mission

We help you increase your awareness on Personal Finance Matters. Through a series of blogs, tools, workbooks, and tips, we aim to give you better control of your finances. Managing your own money and making it work for you is as important, if not more, as earning money.
